Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22nd March, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Trivio ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, protects, and discloses information about users ("you") of the Trivio Android mobile application ("App"). This policy applies to all users of the App and is designed to comply with applicable privacy regulations including the Indian Information Technology Act, 2000, its rules, and global standards such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Google Play Developer Policies.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information: We do not require users to provide personal identifiable information (PII) to use the core features of the app. However, if users choose to contact us via email or opt-in to communication features, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • Support correspondence history

b) Non-Personal Information: We may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device type, operating system version, and language preferences
  • App usage data, including time spent on quizzes, interaction history, and scores
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information
  • Anonymized analytics data (e.g., user engagement trends)

c) Third-Party SDKs and Services: The App uses third-party tools and SDKs such as:

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand user behavior and improve app experience
  • AdMob – to serve advertisements

These providers may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review them:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Practices

2. How We Use Collected Information

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize the quiz experience
  • To analyze app performance and improve functionality
  • To detect and prevent technical issues, abuse, and fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • To communicate with users who reach out to us

3. Data Retention

We retain non-personal and usage data as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. Users may delete app data at any time by uninstalling the app from their device. If you email us, your message and address may be retained for customer service purposes unless you request deletion.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent user personal information. We may share non-personal, anonymized usage data with trusted third-party analytics services. We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or court order, or to protect our rights, users, and operations.

5. Children’s Privacy

Trivio is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from minors. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. This includes secure servers, encrypted traffic, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
